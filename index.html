Who I Am.

Story

A communicator who turns complexity into clarity.

I am Ernestine Joy A. Bataller, a Development Communication professional based in Bicol, Philippines, with a distinctive ability to operate effectively across vastly different communication landscapes — from the structured rigor of international marketing consulting to the fast-paced pressures of primetime television production.

My academic foundation at Camarines Sur Polytechnic Colleges gave me a multidisciplinary lens: I was trained not only to craft compelling narratives but to understand the systems behind information flow, audience behavior, and institutional communication.

Whether I am auditing global audience metrics for a Canadian consulting firm, coordinating the logistics of a live drama production at GMA Network, or leading student governance at scale — I bring the same principle to every role: communication must be purposeful, precise, and deeply human.

My competitive edge lies in the intersection of strategic thinking and storytelling. I have stood on national stages as a Best News Anchor and Best Script awardee, demonstrating that craft, preparation, and authenticity are the pillars of impactful communication.
